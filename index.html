What we do

Give Back Memphis works with skilled professionals to provide pro bono, consulting services to the Memphis non-profit community.

Offering expertise

Operating on skills-based volunteering, we have very efficient and professional volunteers who want to assist nonprofits.

Consulting projects

We match the talents and skills of our volunteer consultants with the business needs of each nonprofit.

Strengthening our community

We serve non-profit organizations operating in Shelby County and registered in Tennessee as a 501c3.

We are continuously
growing.

To date Give Back Memphis has worked with multiple nonprofits, often on more than one project. In the first half of 2020, Give Back Memphis launched new consultancies and designed specific consulting services to meet the implications of operating during a global pandemic.
