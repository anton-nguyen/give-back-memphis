Clients

Who are our clients?

Any non-profit organization registered in the State of Tennessee as a 501c3, operating in Shelby County.

Why participate?

Give Back Memphis provides nonprofits with vetted, consultant volunteers who can provide a variety of different professional services in the areas of governance, planning, programming, marketing, fundraising, finance, human resources and coaching.

How does it work?

Applications for potential clients are accepted on a rolling basis. Either the Executive Director or Board Chair must reach out to Give Back Memphis for an initial meeting to determine if there is a reasonable fit with the nonprofit’s stated needs and available GBM consultant expertise.

What's the commitment?

Defining expectations

  • Board Chair and Executive Director both sign off to the project scope and expected deliverables at the beginning of the consultancy.
  • Board and staff commit to dedicating appropriate time to ensure project’s success (approximately 10-12 hours a month).

Client agreements

  • Client agrees to provide all relevant background documentation to the consulting team.
  • Client completes evaluations requested by Give Back Memphis.
