Impact & Evaluation

We are intentional

Give Back Memphis aims to create positive impact for both its non-profit clients and volunteer consultants.

Evaluation process

Since launching in 2016, Give Back Memphis has instituted a rigorous evaluation process and ongoing impact analysis of it services with Dr. Steven Ross, Evaluation Director of the John Hopkins Center for Research and Reform in Education.

Our statistics

Some of the key findings from Dr. Ross' most recent report include:

96 %

of the nonprofits agreed or strongly agreed with the recommendations made by Give Back consultants

84 %

of clients agreed or strongly agreed that the consultants’ recommendations and deliverables were practical and actionable

100 %

of clients agreed or strongly agreed that the consultants’ dedication of time and resources were appropriate for their needs

100 %

of the consultants agreed or strongly agreed that they enjoyed their experiences
